## Prompt Templates

PromptHub supports dynamic templates with variables:

```
Write a {{length}} blog post about {{topic}} in a {{tone}} tone.
Target audience: {{audience}}
Include: {{requirements}}
```

When you use this template, PromptHub prompts you to fill in:
- `length`: short, medium, long
- `topic`: your subject matter
- `tone`: professional, casual, humorous
- `audience`: your target readers
- `requirements`: specific elements to include
