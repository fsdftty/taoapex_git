# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is TaoApex?
TaoApex is an AI-powered productivity platform that combines three tools: Imagine (AI headshot generator), Talk (AI companion with memory), and PromptHub (prompt manager). Visit [https://taoapex.com/](https://taoapex.com/) to get started.

### Is TaoApex free?
Yes! All three products offer generous free tiers. No credit card required.

### What languages does TaoApex support?
TaoApex is available in 32 languages including English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, and many more.

### Is TaoApex open source?
No. TaoApex is proprietary software. This GitHub repository contains documentation and community resources only. The source code is not available.

## Imagine (AI Headshots)

### How many free AI headshots do I get?
You get 3 free AI-generated headshots when you sign up.

### What photo formats are supported?
JPG, PNG, and WebP formats are supported. We recommend uploading the highest quality photo you have.

### Are my photos stored on your servers?
No. Your uploaded photos are processed and immediately deleted. We do not store your original photos.

### Can I use the AI headshots commercially?
Yes. All images generated by Imagine are yours to use for any purpose, including commercial use.

### What makes a good input photo?
- Clear, front-facing shot
- Good lighting (natural light is best)
- No sunglasses or hats covering the face
- High resolution for best results

## Talk (AI Companion)

### How is Talk different from ChatGPT?
The main difference is **memory**. ChatGPT forgets everything after each conversation. Talk remembers your entire conversation history, building a continuous relationship over time.

### Is Talk a replacement for therapy?
**No.** Talk is an AI companion that can provide emotional support, but it is not a licensed mental health professional. If you're experiencing mental health issues, please seek professional help.

### How long does Talk remember conversations?
- Free plan: 30 days of memory
- Plus plan: 1 year of memory
- Pro plan: Lifetime memory

### Is my conversation data private?
Yes. All conversations are encrypted. We do not share your data with third parties, and no human moderators read your private conversations.

### Can Talk make voice calls?
Voice chat is available on Plus and Pro plans.

## PromptHub (Prompt Manager)

### How many prompts can I store for free?
The free plan allows up to 100 prompts.

### Can I share prompts with my team?
Yes, team sharing is available on Pro and Team plans.

### Does PromptHub have a browser extension?
Yes! The browser extension is available for Chrome and Firefox, allowing you to access your prompts from any website.

### Can I export my prompts?
Yes. You can export your entire prompt library in JSON or CSV format.

### Does PromptHub work with [specific AI tool]?
PromptHub is AI-platform agnostic. Your prompts can be used with any AI tool including ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, and more.
