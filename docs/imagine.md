## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use a clear, front-facing photo** - The AI works best with well-lit face shots
2. **Avoid sunglasses or hats** - Faces should be clearly visible
3. **Higher resolution input = better output** - Upload the highest quality photo you have
4. **Try multiple styles** - Different styles suit different purposes
